About the practice

You deserve a dentist who treats you like family.

Life's busy enough without dental pain or complicated treatment plans. At Farley Family Dental, we focus on the things that actually matter to you: a trustworthy dentist who explains things clearly, keeps appointments on time, and never pressures you into work you don't need.

Dr. John Farley and Dr. Kevin Farley have spent more than three decades caring for the people of Payson — and many of our patients have been with us their whole lives.

What we do

Comprehensive care for every smile.

From your six-month cleaning to a full smile makeover, we handle nearly everything in-house — so you don't have to bounce between specialists.

Cleanings & Prevention

Routine exams, professional cleanings, fluoride, and sealants. The foundation of a healthy mouth for life.

Fillings & Restorations

Tooth-colored composite fillings that blend in seamlessly. Quick, comfortable, and built to last.

Crowns & Bridges

Restore broken or missing teeth with custom-fit crowns and bridges that look and feel natural.

Root Canals

Pain relief, not pain creation. Modern root canal therapy is faster and gentler than you remember.

Extractions

When a tooth has to go, we make it as comfortable and uneventful as possible — including wisdom teeth.

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening that's safe, fast, and dramatic — without the drugstore-aisle guesswork.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Veneers, bonding, and smile design. Subtle improvements or full transformations — your call.

Emergency Care

Toothache, broken crown, knocked-out tooth? Call us — we make room for emergencies the same day when we can.
