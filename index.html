Common questions

Things patients ask us.

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes — we welcome new patients of all ages. Call (801) 465-3256 or book online and we'll get you in within a couple of weeks.

What insurance do you accept?

We work with most major PPO plans including Delta Dental, Blue Cross, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, United Healthcare, Guardian, and Humana. Don't see yours? Call us — we verify benefits for free before your visit.

Do you see kids?

Absolutely. We're a family practice — three generations of patients are common here. We start seeing kids around age 1–2 for short, friendly visits to build comfort with the dentist.

How long is a typical appointment?

A cleaning and check-up runs about 60 minutes. Just a check-up takes 30 minutes. We start on time and respect yours — most patients are in and out without a long wait.

What if I have a dental emergency?

Call us first thing in the morning and we'll do our best to fit you in the same day. For after-hours emergencies, leave a message and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

I'm nervous about the dentist. Can you help?

Many of our patients started out nervous. We move at your pace, explain everything before we do it, and never pressure you into work you're not ready for. Tell us at booking and we'll plan accordingly.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We offer in-house payment plans for larger treatments and accept CareCredit financing (often 0% interest for qualifying patients). HSA and FSA cards work too.

How often should I come in?

Standard recommendation is every six months for a cleaning and check-up. If you're at higher risk for cavities or gum disease, we may suggest every 3–4 months — always your call.
